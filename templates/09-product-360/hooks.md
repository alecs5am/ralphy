# Product 360 — Hook Library

The first 2 seconds decide whether the viewer commits. A product 360 hook is always a **lighting move** combined with a **product entry** — not just "camera on, product visible". Below are 12 hooks, with the situations they fit, prompt seeds, and timing.

Use one per spot. Match the hook to the product category and price tier — a luxury watch hook on a $19 phone case reads as oversold, and vice versa.

---

## 1. Dramatic Spotlight Snap

**Use for:** Luxury watches, jewelry, premium fragrance, anything that needs to communicate "this is expensive in the first half-second".

**Beat (0-2s):** Black frame. Single hard spotlight snaps on at 30-45° above the product. Product appears already in position — no movement yet, just sudden hard illumination. Specular highlight catches a hero surface (crystal face, gem facet, polished steel). Brief pause to let the viewer absorb.

**Prompt seed:** `Pure black frame. Single hard spotlight snaps on from upper-left at 35° above subject, illuminating [product] in dramatic isolation. Hard shadow falls right. Specular highlight glints off [hero surface]. Camera locked, product still. Cinematic, high-contrast, luxury.`

**Pairs with:** Slow turntable rotation, cinematic strings, single sonic chime at hit.

---

## 2. Particle Materialization

**Use for:** Tech, jewelry, beauty / cosmetics, premium goods. Anything where "magic" reads as quality.

**Beat (0-2s):** Empty dark frame. Tiny golden / silver / colored particles drift and swirl. Particles converge toward center, accelerate, coalesce, and solidify into the product. Final 0.3s: subtle dust drift settles around the now-formed product.

**Prompt seed:** `Empty dark frame, fine golden particles drift and swirl in shallow depth of field. Particles accelerate inward, converge, and solidify into [product]. Soft glow lingers around edges as remaining particles settle. Camera static, slight rim light from behind.`

**Pairs with:** Ambient electronic pulse, harmonic chime, slow medium rotation.

---

## 3. Crash & Shockwave

**Use for:** Athletic footwear, automotive parts, robust tech, anything that needs to communicate durability or impact-energy.

**Beat (0-2s):** Empty surface (white seamless or polished black). Product drops from above frame, hits the surface with weight. Subtle dust ring / shockwave ripples outward. Brief micro-bounce, settle. Product comes to rest at hero angle.

**Prompt seed:** `White seamless studio surface, top-down lighting. [Product] drops from upper frame, lands center with weight. Subtle dust shockwave ripples outward in a ring. Micro-bounce, settles into three-quarter hero angle. Sharp impact, not cartoonish.`

**Pairs with:** Tight percussion hit on impact, modern electronic underscore, fast rotation follow.

---

## 4. Peel / Unwrap Reveal

**Use for:** New launches, packaged goods, anything where the unboxing-feel sells. Footwear, electronics, cosmetics in cellophane, premium boxes.

**Beat (0-2s):** Translucent plastic / tissue / wrapping covers the product. Wrapping peels back smoothly from one corner, retracting out of frame. Product emerges pristine, fresh, untouched. Wrapping fades / drifts away.

**Prompt seed:** `[Product] wrapped in translucent protective film. Film peels back smoothly from upper-right corner, retracts out of frame, revealing pristine [product] beneath. Soft warm light. Slight motion blur on the peeling film. Studio surface.`

**Pairs with:** Soft fabric / paper foley sound, anticipatory warm music, slow lift into rotation.

---

## 5. Macro-to-Wide Pull-Out

**Use for:** Products where a single detail (stitching, leather grain, brushed metal, fabric weave) IS the value prop. Bags, watches, premium fashion, artisan goods.

**Beat (0-2s):** Extreme macro on a single texture — leather grain, stitching detail, knit pattern. Holds for ~0.8s so the texture registers. Camera pulls back smoothly, revealing more of the surface, then the full product silhouette at hero angle.

**Prompt seed:** `Extreme macro: [specific texture, e.g., diagonal saddle-stitching in waxed thread on tan leather]. Hold 0.8s. Camera pulls back smoothly and continuously, surface widens into full [product] at three-quarter hero angle. Warm grazing key light, shallow depth.`

**Pairs with:** Subtle leather / fabric foley, warm orchestral or piano underscore, slow rotation continuation.

---

## 6. Halo Light Flare

**Use for:** Jewelry, crystal / glass, polished metal, perfume bottles. Anything where a clean light reflection IS the luxury signal.

**Beat (0-2s):** Product already in frame, dimly lit. A brilliant arc of light sweeps across the product's hero surface — like a lens flare arcing through a diamond facet or sapphire crystal. Light reaches peak intensity at center frame, then settles into normal exposure.

**Prompt seed:** `[Product, e.g., diamond ring] in soft dim ambient. Brilliant arc of light flare sweeps left-to-right across [hero surface], peaking at center frame. Light catches faceting / reflection cleanly, no chromatic aberration. Settles into clean studio exposure.`

**Pairs with:** Crystalline bell chime, cinematic strings sustain, slow turntable.

---

## 7. Slow Lift / Float

**Use for:** Premium cosmetics, jewelry, perfume, anything where weightlessness reads as luxury.

**Beat (0-2s):** Product rests on surface. With subtle physics, product slowly lifts — ascending 5-15cm into the air, gently rotating as it rises. Surface beneath remains in soft focus. Product comes to a hovering rest, mid-frame, ready for rotation phase.

**Prompt seed:** `[Product] rests on white surface. With graceful weightlessness, [product] lifts upward, ascending 10cm while beginning a slow rotation. Soft shadow on surface remains. Product hovers center-frame at end. Smooth, dreamlike, cinematic.`

**Pairs with:** Soft piano / harp, gentle whoosh on lift, slow rotation continuation.

---

## 8. Half-Spin Surprise Reveal

**Use for:** Products with asymmetric / hidden detail — embossed branding on a back panel, engraving on the underside, a contrasting interior color, a textured back.

**Beat (0-2s):** Product in front-facing hero angle, fully visible. Rotates 180° smoothly, revealing the back / hidden side. The back is the surprise — a detail the front didn't show. Product holds at 180°.

**Prompt seed:** `[Product] front-facing on neutral surface. Smoothly rotates 180° clockwise. Back panel reveals [hidden detail — embossing / engraving / color]. Holds at half-rotation, hero detail clearly visible. Even key light maintains exposure across rotation.`

**Pairs with:** Tight rhythmic underscore, soft mechanical click at 180°, full-360 rotation continuation OR cut to macro.

---

## 9. Liquid Pour / Color Bloom

**Use for:** Perfume, beverages, cosmetics with visible color, transparent containers.

**Beat (0-2s):** Empty / clear bottle. Liquid begins to pour or appear from above — golden fragrance, colored serum, beverage. Fills the container partially or fully. Color saturation peaks. Light catches the liquid surface and refracts through the glass.

**Prompt seed:** `Clear glass [bottle / container] empty on white surface. Golden / [color] liquid pours in from above, fills container 70%, settles. Liquid surface settles flat with subtle meniscus. Side back-light shows color saturation and refraction through glass. Camera static.`

**Pairs with:** Subtle pour foley, light glassy chime, slow rotation continuation.

---

## 10. Light Sweep / Color Cascade

**Use for:** Multi-color products, products with iridescent / holographic / gradient finishes, beauty palettes.

**Beat (0-2s):** Product in frame under flat ambient. A sweeping directional light passes across the product (like a moving softbox), revealing color gradient / iridescence / shifting tone as the light passes. Light settles into final position.

**Prompt seed:** `[Product with color gradient / iridescent finish] under flat ambient. Sweeping directional key light passes left-to-right across product over 1.5s, revealing iridescent shifts / gradient transitions. Light settles at 35° above-left. Specular highlights now stable.`

**Pairs with:** Modern electronic underscore, ascending tonal sweep, medium rotation.

---

## 11. Pedestal Reveal

**Use for:** Architectural / sculptural products — perfume bottles with statement caps, watch displays, premium stationery, sculptural cosmetics.

**Beat (0-2s):** Empty pedestal / surface in soft light. Product rises smoothly from below frame on the pedestal — like a museum display elevator. Reaches full height, gently bobs to rest. Optional: subtle ground-level mist / light glow during rise.

**Prompt seed:** `Empty white circular pedestal in soft studio light. [Product] rises smoothly from below frame, ascends to full pedestal height over 1.5s, settles. Subtle ground-level light glow during rise. Camera locked, slight low angle to emphasize pedestal.`

**Pairs with:** Cinematic strings build, gentle whoosh + final settle thud, slow turntable from pedestal.

---

## 12. Drop-In with Splash / Powder

**Use for:** Cosmetics with powder / pigment, food / beverage with ingredients, beauty serums, anything where "ingredients" or "freshness" is the story.

**Beat (0-2s):** Empty surface. Product drops down with motion, accompanied by a halo of related material — pigment puff, splash droplets, ingredient particles (coffee beans, leaves, etc.) — that radiate outward then settle around the product. Product lands at hero angle.

**Prompt seed:** `Clean light surface. [Product] drops from above with a surrounding halo of [pigment puff / water droplets / ingredient particles] radiating outward as it lands. Particles settle around base. Product comes to rest at three-quarter hero angle. Soft warm key.`

**Pairs with:** Tactile soft impact + particle settle, warm lifestyle music, medium rotation.

---

## Hook selection cheat-sheet

| Product category | Default hook |
|---|---|
| Luxury watch | Dramatic Spotlight Snap |
| Jewelry / fine | Halo Light Flare |
| Premium tech / earbuds | Particle Materialization |
| Athletic footwear | Crash & Shockwave |
| Premium footwear / fashion | Peel Reveal |
| Leather goods / bags | Macro-to-Wide Pull |
| Cosmetics / serum | Slow Lift OR Drop-In with Powder |
| Perfume / fragrance | Halo Flare OR Liquid Pour |
| Multi-color / iridescent | Light Sweep |
| Sculptural / statement | Pedestal Reveal |
| Asymmetric back-detail | Half-Spin Reveal |
| Beverage | Liquid Pour OR Drop-In with Splash |
