# Hooks — 0-2s patterns

The first 2 seconds are the entire retention bet for UGC selfie review. The persona's face must already be in frame, looking directly at camera, with the product visible OR the first reaction line already mid-utterance.

All hooks below are i2v anchors — the first-frame image carries the persona master + product master locked refs.

## Hook 1 — "Wait... look at this." (validated, source project)

**Frame**: face top-third, product at chin, label readable, internals visible behind glass.
**Action 0-1s**: 1-2 RELAXED slow nail-taps on product wall (NOT excited drumming).
**Line 1-2s**: slow chill with pause — `"Wait...... wait, look at this."`
**Voice register**: light vocal fry + soft natural breaths between words. Long pause between "Wait" and "wait" is intentional.

**Why it works**: the "wait" stalls the scroll without spoiling the payoff. The nail-tap is auditory specificity bait (TikTok eats it). The product-at-chin geometry stays >30cm from cheek so kling doesn't drift.

## Hook 2 — Direct callout to internal specificity

**Frame**: extreme macro on product internals (charms / texture / inclusions), persona's mouth + chin out of focus background.
**Action 0-1s**: jar / tube slowly rotates 15-20 deg; sunlight glints off one internal element.
**Line 0-2s**: amused soft breath — `"Okay... what is THAT?"` (where "THAT" = the named specificity element, e.g. "a tiny pony")
**Voice register**: hushed, amused, conspiratorial.

**Why it works**: opens on the specificity payoff frame instead of waiting for it. Higher information density per second. Best for products with a clear visible micro-feature.

## Hook 3 — Pre-roll dab + reaction

**Frame**: cheek medium shot, glitter-coated fingertip already mid-approach to right cheekbone.
**Action 0-1s**: fingertip dabs cheek ONCE softly; face turns slightly left then right showing the shimmer in light.
**Line 1-2s**: slow amazed with breathing space — `"It goes on like... oh my god, look at my cheek."`
**Voice register**: genuine quiet surprise, NOT theatrical.

**Why it works**: opens on the application beat rather than the unboxing. Skips the obvious "show the bottle" first frame. Best as the SECOND clip's hook if the first clip was a product-reveal.

## Hook 4 — Cosmic dub-style cold open (untested in source)

**Frame**: 1-2 frames of black, then SNAP cut into face direct-to-camera holding product.
**Action 0-1s**: persona BLINKS once, lips part.
**Line 1-2s**: deadpan flat — `"So this is supposed to do WHAT exactly."`
**Voice register**: dry, deadpan, no excitement.

**Why it works**: the 1-frame black flash is unusual for UGC and stalls the scroll involuntarily. Pair with deadpan delivery for ironic register. Use sparingly — overuse reads as commercial.

## What NOT to do in the first 2 seconds

- **No product at face / cheek / mouth**. Kling drifts to powder-compact stamping. Keep at arms length.
- **No music in the kling prompt**. Music is overlaid in Remotion at volume 0.18; baked-in kling music conflicts.
- **No theatrical surprise**. The format is calm curious GRWM, not "look-at-this-omg" influencer-tone. Voice-tag block enforces.
- **No text overlay** on the kling clip itself. Captions / brand tags go on the Remotion track if at all.
- **No `--audio: false` for English**. You will reshoot. (Memory: take1 of source project burned $0.42 here.)
