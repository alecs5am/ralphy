# Voice mode: `deadpan-rant`

**When:** Single-idea B2B / education / opinion monologue. Hormozi / Codie Sanchez register. The point is the words, not the performance.

## ElevenLabs settings

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| `stability` | 0.55 |
| `similarity_boost` | 0.85 |
| `style` | 0.10 |
| `use_speaker_boost` | true |

## Sample VO

> Most people fail because they pick the wrong thing to be persistent about. They confuse stubbornness with focus. The two are not the same — one wins, the other just feels like winning.

## Pacing target

~2.4 words/sec. Pauses at every period — don't ride through them. Slight breath audible between sentences (don't EQ it out).

## Do

- Single-take energy. Re-cuts kill the deadpan.
- Land each period with conviction.

## Don't

- Smile-talk. No "broadcast warm" inflection.
- Music bed louder than -22 LUFS — it competes with the flat delivery.
